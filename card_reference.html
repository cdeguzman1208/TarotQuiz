Tarot Card Reference

The Sovereign (The Emperor)
Symbol: Dragon Throne

Meaning: Authority, control, stability, leadership. The Dragon Throne represents the ultimate symbol of power and the mandate of heaven.

The Empress
Symbol: Phoenix Crown

Meaning: Fertility, beauty, abundance, creation. The Phoenix Crown signifies the Empress's role as a nurturer and the embodiment of grace.

The Sage (The Hierophant)
Symbol: Jade Scroll

Meaning: Wisdom, spiritual guidance, tradition, knowledge. The Jade Scroll contains ancient wisdom and teachings from celestial beings.

The Hero (The Chariot)
Symbol: Tiger Chariot

Meaning: Victory, determination, willpower, triumph. The Tiger Chariot, driven by a legendary warrior, symbolizes unstoppable progress and conquest.

The Weaver (The Wheel of Fortune)
Symbol: Lunar Loom

Meaning: Fate, cycles, change, destiny. The Lunar Loom, used by the Weaver Goddess, determines the threads of life and destiny.

The Guardian (Strength)
Symbol: Kirin

Meaning: Courage, strength, inner power, resilience. The Kirin, a mythical creature, represents the ultimate protector and bearer of justice.

The Enlightened One (The Hermit)
Symbol: Lotus Lantern

Meaning: Inner wisdom, solitude, enlightenment, introspection. The Lotus Lantern lights the path to spiritual enlightenment and inner peace.

The Lovers
Symbol: Yin-Yang Birds

Meaning: Union, harmony, love, partnership. The Yin-Yang Birds, always in pairs, symbolize the perfect balance and harmony of love.

The Seer (The High Priestess)
Symbol: Moon Mirror

Meaning: Intuition, mystery, subconscious, hidden knowledge. The Moon Mirror reflects hidden truths and unseen realms, guiding those who seek deeper understanding.

The Wanderer (The Fool)
Symbol: Crane

Meaning: New beginnings, innocence, adventure, spontaneity. The Crane, a symbol of longevity and exploration, embarks on a journey filled with potential.

The Arbiter (Justice)
Symbol: Sword of Truth

Meaning: Fairness, truth, justice, balance. The Sword of Truth, wielded by the Arbiter, cuts through deception and upholds justice.

The Rebirth (Death)
Symbol: Cherry Blossom

Meaning: Transformation, endings, renewal, new beginnings. The Cherry Blossom, representing the fleeting nature of life, also symbolizes rebirth and renewal.

The Tempest (The Tower)
Symbol: Thunder Drum

Meaning: Upheaval, sudden change, revelation, chaos. The Thunder Drum, when struck, brings forth storms that cleanse and transform.

The Alchemist (Temperance)
Symbol: Yin-Yang Cauldron

Meaning: Balance, harmony, patience, moderation. The Yin-Yang Cauldron blends opposites to create harmony and balance in all things.

The Illusionist (The Devil)
Symbol: Demon Mask

Meaning: Temptation, illusion, bondage, materialism. The Demon Mask reveals the seductive nature of illusions and earthly desires.

The Star Maiden (The Star)
Symbol: Starry Fan

Meaning: Hope, inspiration, guidance, renewal. The Starry Fan, wielded by the Star Maiden, brings hope and guidance to those who are lost.

The Moon Guardian (The Moon)
Symbol: Moon Pearl

Meaning: Illusion, intuition, dreams, the unconscious. The Moon Pearl, a gift from the Moon Guardian, illuminates the shadows of the mind and the realm of dreams.

The Sun Lord (The Sun)
Symbol: Sun Disk

Meaning: Joy, success, vitality, enlightenment. The Sun Disk, representing the Sun Lord, radiates warmth, vitality, and the promise of a new day.

The World Tree (The World)
Symbol: World Tree

Meaning: Completion, wholeness, fulfillment, harmony. The World Tree, connecting heaven and earth, represents the culmination of a journey and the attainment of harmony.

The Awakening (Judgement)
Symbol: Phoenix Rising

Meaning: Rebirth, awakening, transformation, renewal. The Phoenix Rising from its ashes symbolizes the awakening to a higher state of being and consciousness.

The Heavenly Court (The Hanged Man)
Symbol: Heavenly Court

Meaning: Sacrifice, suspension, letting go, new perspectives. The Heavenly Court, where gods convene, represents the need for reflection and the willingness to see things from a higher perspective.
